My Study Strategies

  • Use the Pomodoro technique — 25 minutes study, 5 minutes break.
  • Summarize each topic after class to improve memory.
  • Watch educational YouTube channels on the topics you are studying on .
  • Use flashcards for key definitions and helps with active recall.
  • Ask questions early and participate in group discussions.
